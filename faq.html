Construction Services

How long does a typical construction project take? +

Project timelines vary based on the scope and complexity:

  • Small residential (1000-1500 sq ft): 6-8 months
  • Large residential (2000+ sq ft): 10-14 months
  • Commercial projects: Timeline depends on specifications

We provide a detailed project timeline during the planning phase and keep you updated on progress throughout.

Do you provide a warranty on construction work? +

Yes, we provide workmanship warranty on all our construction projects. The warranty period is specified in the project agreement and typically covers structural defects and workmanship issues. Material warranties are as per the manufacturer's terms.

What is the payment structure for construction projects? +

Our payment structure is milestone-based:

  • Advance: 10-20% at project commencement
  • Foundation stage: 15-20%
  • Superstructure: 20-25%
  • Finishing stage: 25-30%
  • Handover: Remaining balance

Exact percentages may vary based on project specifics and will be detailed in the agreement.

Interior Design

Do you handle complete interior design or specific rooms only? +

We offer both options! Whether you need a complete home interior makeover or want to redesign specific spaces like kitchen, bedroom, or living room, we can help. Our interior design services are flexible and tailored to your needs and budget.

What is included in your interior design package? +

Our comprehensive interior design package includes:

  • Initial consultation and space analysis
  • 2D and 3D design visualizations
  • Material and color palette selection
  • Furniture layout and recommendations
  • Lighting design
  • Execution and project management
  • Quality checks and final walkthrough
Can I see designs before execution starts? +

Absolutely! We provide detailed 3D renderings and visualizations of your space before any execution begins. You'll have the opportunity to review, suggest changes, and approve the final design. We believe in complete transparency and ensuring you're 100% satisfied with the design before we start work.

Vastu & Consultation

Do you incorporate Vastu principles in your designs? +

Yes, we offer Vastu-compliant design solutions for clients who wish to incorporate traditional Vastu Shastra principles into their homes or commercial spaces. Our team includes Vastu experts who can guide you on optimal placement of rooms, entrances, and other elements as per Vastu guidelines while maintaining modern aesthetics.

Can I get only consultation without construction services? +

Yes, we offer standalone consultation services. Whether you need expert advice on construction planning, design concepts, material selection, cost estimation, or Vastu guidance, our consultation services are available independently. This is ideal for clients who want professional guidance but plan to execute through other channels.
